Camp site

It is 4:00PM. Your bag sits in front of you. What do you do?

You take your bag without checking or you check your bag again for needed supplies.

You Take your bag without checking, leaving your campsite, walking towards the cave entrance. or You You check your bag for needed supplies, noticing you are missing a few items, but only one more item will fit in your bag.

Venturing into the cave, you have already been caught with the decision of going right, or left.

Choosing your full thermos, you now have a small supply of water.

Choosing the path to the right, you are caught with a tight gap, with this, you hold your supplies to your side as you begin to squeeze through.

Choosing the left path, you are met with a wide gap with jointed and jagged sides and ceilings.

After squeezing through the tight gap, you turn to notice your bag has ripped open, leaving a box of matches behind and a box of plastic lunch bags.

Stepping slowly through the jagged cave tunnel, you fall and scratch your ankles, causing you to bleed and using some of your supply.

Continuing down the right path, you hear something collide. As you turn you notice the small gap has closed you in.

Continuing down the left path, you hear a few flaps of bat wings flutter by your head. Looking down you notice a pair of eyes gleaming towards you.

Scared, you shimmy down the right path, slowly drinking your water in fear as you deplete your supplies.
